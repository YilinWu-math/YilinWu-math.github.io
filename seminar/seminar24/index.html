About

Pre-Calabi-Yau algebras were introduced by M. Kontsevich and Y. Vlassopoulos around 2015. However, these structures (or equivalent ones) have appeared in other works under different names, such as $V_{\infty}$-algebras by T. Tradler and M. Zeinalian, $A_{\infty}$-algebras with boundary or noncommutative divisors in the work of P. Seidel, or weak Calabi-Yau structures in earlier talks by Kontsevich.
