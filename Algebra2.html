Algèbre 2: Commutative algebra and Representation theory

Course Description

This course provides an introduction to the theory of commutative algebra and representation theory. This will include

  • Rings and ideals
  • Modules over rings
  • Localization
  • Properties of important classes of rings, such as discrete valuation rings and Dedekind domains
  • Quivers and their representations
  • Representations of groups and category theory.
