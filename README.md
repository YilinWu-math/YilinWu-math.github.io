# Yilin Wu | Personal Academic Homepage

This repository hosts the personal academic homepage of **Yilin Wu**.

**Official website:** <https://yilinwu-math.github.io/>

## About

Yilin Wu obtained a PhD under the direction of **Prof. Bernhard Keller** and **Prof. Guodong Zhou**. Yilin is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the **University of Luxembourg**, working with **Prof. Sarah Scherotzke**. Previously, Yilin was a postdoc at the **University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)**, working with **Prof. Xiao-Wu Chen**.

## Research interests

- Cluster algebras, categorification, combinatorics, and links to topology
- Representation theory, dg categories, and noncommutative algebraic geometry
- Quiver varieties, ∞-categories, derived geometry, and Calabi-Yau structures
- Higgs categories, relative cluster categories, silting reduction, and ice quiver mutation

## Publications and preprints

Selected works include:

- **Silting reduction, relative AGK's construction and Higgs construction**
- **Group actions on Higgs categories and relative cluster categories**
- **Relative cluster categories and Higgs categories with infinite-dimensional morphism spaces**, joint with Bernhard Keller
- **GAGA type results for singularity categories**, joint with Jinyi Xu and Guodong Zhou, *Journal of Pure and Applied Algebra* 229 (2025)
- **Relative cluster categories and Higgs categories**, *Advances in Mathematics* 424 (2023)
- **Categorification of ice quiver mutation**, *Mathematische Zeitschrift* 304 (2023)
- **From short exact sequences of abelian categories to short exact sequences of homotopy categories and derived categories**, joint with Guodong Zhou, *Electronic Research Archive* 30 (2022)
