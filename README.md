## About

Yilin Wu obtained a PhD under the direction of **Prof. Bernhard Keller** and **Prof. Guodong Zhou**. Yilin is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the **University of Luxembourg**, working with **Prof. Sarah Scherotzke**. Previously, Yilin was a postdoc at the **University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)**, working with **Prof. Xiao-Wu Chen**.
